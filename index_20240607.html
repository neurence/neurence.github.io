Comparison of outputs with/without pre-denoising

We've been training reduced latency versions of the encoder, and also different datasets/data augmentations:

  • WavLM Large (pre-encoder + 24 Transformer Layers, which is what's currently implemented in the phone)
  • WavLM pre-encoder only (No Transformer layers, reduced latency)
  • WavLM Base+ (pre-encoder + 12 Transformer Layers, reduced latency)
  • WavLM Large (pre-encoder + 24 Transformer Layers), trained with speed augmentation
  • WavLM Large (pre-encoder + 24 Transformer Layers), trained with the Mozilla CV Corpus, a massive crowdsourced speech dataset (5,000 hours of low-quality speech)
