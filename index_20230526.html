wav2vec2wav: A Wav2Vec-based autoencoder

Wav2Vec2 is a fully-causal Transformer network producing rich embeddings from raw speech.

It has been used for a number of speech tasks, including speaker identification and diarization, emotion detection, and, of course, speech to text.

We're currently running experiments to use a pretrained Wav2Vec2 model in conjunction with a decoder of our own design to resynthesize audio. The decoder models are at an early stage of training -- training takes about seven days.
