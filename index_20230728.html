Denoiser Fine-Tuning

Experiments are in progress, at an early stage of training, fine-tuning our "best" denoiser network in the full pipeline (i.e. used as a processor for the transformer autoencoder).

We'll have some preliminary results next week.

Real-time streaming test harness

We've developed, and are starting to use, a realtime test-harness which streams microphone input through the models and outputs to the speaker/headphone.

The latency, without optimisation, is around 1 second. We're looking at latency/quality trade-offs, and network latency improvements, based on This paper from Dolby Labs.

Additional Compute Workstations

We've placed an order for two AI workstations. Each machine has two 24Gb NVIDIA 4090 GPUs.

In addition to speeding up training time, these machines will allow us to train the large transformer networks that our current 12Gb NVIDIA 1080s and TITANs can't handle.
